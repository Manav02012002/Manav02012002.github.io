I am a Research Intern at the University of Glasgow working with Dr. Chris Bouchard in the School of Physics and Astronomy on Generative AI in lattice QCD. Previously I worked on QCD colour-flow observables with Professor Andy Buckley for my MSc Project at Glasgow.

I have a Master of Science (MSc) degree in Theoretical Physics from the University of Glasgow and a Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry from St. Joseph's College (Autonomous), Bengaluru City University, Bangalore, India.

My Research Interests lie in Theoretical and Computational Physics, with dabblings in Particle Phenomenology as well. My Primary Research Interests lie in both Perturbative and Non-Perturbative regimes of Quantum Chromodynamics, Holography and just Gauge Theories in general. On the computer side of things, I am broadly interested in High Performance Computing and AI/ML but more specifically in Generative AI applied to Physics.

I am available for PhD positions in Particle Physics - particularly Lattice QCD, QCD Phenomenology, AI in Lattice Field theory and other related projects. If a position is available, please feel free to contact me!
